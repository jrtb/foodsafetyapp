Food Safety Careers

The Problem

  • New regulations calling for increased investment in food safety

  • Calls for a more skilled, qualified workforce readily prepared with the education to tackle safety problems

What Do I Need?

  • Driven individuals dedicated to keeping a safe food source

  • Interest in health, food, science, regulation, serving the public

  • A degree in Food Science (various food safety certification)

What Will I Get?

  • A degree in food science (from NC State)

  • Learn about the science behind food and how to make it better/safer

  • Career in food safety- anywhere along “production chain”

  • Job security- Food safety is a currently expanding

How do I Get it?

  • Enroll in the Food science program at NCSU

  • Earn a degree in 4 years or less- earn food safety certification at the same time

  • Graduate with a career almost anywhere around the country or locally
