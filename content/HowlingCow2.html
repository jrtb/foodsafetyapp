Buy Howling Cow in Bulk

Our delicious ice cream and other fine dairy products are a great treat for your family and friends, or a welcome addition to social events or business functions. Anyone can buy them.

You can order by phone 8am - 4pm Mon-Fri. Call 919.515.2760.
You can also email a request to wchollif@ncsu.edu

How to Pay Us

We cannot accept cash at Schaub Hall! We do accept Credit Cards and Checks. Please make checks payable to NCSU Creamery. State agencies may request to be invoiced; NC State employees may use inter-department transfers.

Where and When to Pick It Up

Orders may be picked up Monday – Friday, 8:00 am – 4:00 pm at Room 12 in Schaub Hall. The building is on the corner of Dan Allen and Sullivan Drive. A loading dock is provided at the rear of the building.

Please Note
3 gallon tubs of ice cream are -15º F. when purchased. The ideal dipping temperature is +4 to +6º F. The proper atemperation of ice cream tubs will take 24–48 hours in a home freezer.
