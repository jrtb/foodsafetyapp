We need YOU!

Do you want to make an impact? Do you want to help people? Do you want to do something that touches the life of every human being?

If so, consider a career in food safety! The CDC estimates that every year, 1 in 6 Americans will suffer from a foodborne illness. In a food safety career, you would be able to work towards protecting the world from foodborne illnesses.

Since the Food Safety Modernization Act was signed into law in 2014, there is a great need for trained, competent employees in the food industry.

We need YOU to step up and protect our nation. With a degree in Food Science you can help keep your friends, family, and nation safe by preventing foodborne illness outbreaks.
