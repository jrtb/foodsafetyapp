How to get started

Enroll in a Food Science degree program!

There are many scholarship opportunities within the food science field for eager students.
You'll be able to earn a degree in 4 years or less! And at NC State you can earn a Food Safety Manager Certificate while you complete your regular coursework.
  
Most importantly, you'll graduate ready for a career almost anywhere around the country or locally. North Carolina is home to the headquarters for many companies including: Krispy Kreme, Mt. Olive Pickle Company, and Lowes Foods.
